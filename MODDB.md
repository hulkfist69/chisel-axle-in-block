## Full description

**Chisel Encased Axles** finally lets the chisel and the mechanical power network
play nice together.

Vintage Story already lets you hide an axle inside a building block (wrench in
the offhand, block in your main hand, right-click the axle). But the moment you
try to chisel that block, the axle is gone — power and all. This mod fixes that.

Now you can **chisel an encased axle just like any other block** — carve it,
shape it, add multiple materials, use every chisel mode and size — and the axle
underneath keeps right on spinning, still wired into the same network. Build
flush wall shafts, hidden drive lines, decorative gearing, and clean machine
rooms without sacrificing function for looks.

### Features

- **Chisel around a live axle.** Encase an axle in a block, then chisel it. The
  axle keeps spinning and stays on the mechanical network.
- **Full vanilla chiseling.** Multi-material, chisel modes, sizes, rotation — it
  behaves exactly like a normal chiseled block, because it *is* one.
- **Drop a pre-chiseled block straight onto an axle.** Hold a chiseled block,
  wrench in the offhand, right-click the axle — it takes on that exact carved
  shape with the axle preserved underneath. (Same gesture as encasing with a
  plain block.)
- **Nothing is lost.** Break a chiseled axle and you get both the chiseled block
  and the axle back.
- **The shaft still shows.** The spinning wooden axle renders through the parts
  you carve away.

### How to use it

1. Place an axle and connect it to your power network.
2. With a **wrench in your offhand** and a **block in your main hand**, sprint +
   right-click the axle to encase it (vanilla feature).
3. Take your **chisel** (and a **hammer in the offhand**, as usual) and carve the
   block. The axle keeps spinning.

Or skip a step: with a **wrench in the offhand** and a **pre-chiseled block** in
your main hand, sprint + right-click the axle to apply that carved shape directly.

### Compatibility & requirements

- Vintage Story **1.22**.
- Works with the base-game mechanical power and chiseling systems; no
  configuration required.
- Universal (install on client and server).
